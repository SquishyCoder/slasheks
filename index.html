about \x

My pseudonym is slasheks, and I am a former network administrator and current operations specialist (devops) located on the East coast of the United States. Off-hours: infosec and cryptocurrency enthusiast.

I have been involved in the Lisk community since Feb/March 2016. I have learned many things and made numerous friends during this time for which I am grateful. This is something that was made possible because of the Lisk ecosystem. I am very thankful to make it into active delegation and I thank the Lisk community for having me.

As an active delegate, I try to contribute to the ecosystem in any way I can. This can take the form of time, coding (python, shell, etc) as well as advice as needed. My goal is to promote the platform as much as I can. I want to see lisk become a mainstream platform for applications as an alternative to closed source/wall offerings out there.

Before becoming active with Lisk, I spent some of my free time researching cryptocurrencies and investing in those that provided a technical solution to a current/future problem or enhanced what is possible with our current technology.

The Lisk decentralized application platform, it's most powerful component, allows the deployment, distribution and monetisation of decentralized applications and custom blockchains (sidechains) onto the Lisk blockchain.

Max Kordek
